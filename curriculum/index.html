CMU CS Academy Sandbox

Teknowledge utilizes Carnegie Mellon University's CS Academy. Our aim is to expose middle school students to the fun and engaging possibilities of knowing how to code through custom graphic design, interactive art and creating your own video games. We hope you have fun with this curriculum!


If you are planning to use or adapt our curriculum, please email cmu.teknowledge@gmail.com. Don't hesitate to reach out with any feedback, inquiries, or curiosities!
