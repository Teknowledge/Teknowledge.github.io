Our Homewood YMCA Program

  • When? Mondays and Wednesdays, 4:00-5:30 PM
  • For how long? January 18th through April 4th
  • Where? At the Homewood YMCA: 7140 Bennett St, Pittsburgh, PA 15208
  • Who can apply? All students, 6th through 12th grade, who go to school in Pittsburgh.
  • Who leads the program? Students and alumni from Carnegie Mellon University, in partnership with the YMCA and Carnegie Mellon professors
  • How do I get involved? Apply at the link above!

Teknowledge welcomes students of all backgrounds, grades 6th-12th. We are particularly excited to work with 9th & 10th grade students.

The ideal student would:

  • Have an interest in problem solving and learning inside and outside the classroom
  • Be eager to actively seek new opportunities
  • Currently have litle access to existing support structures that will further develop their interest in technology

Students will:

  • Gain experiences with computer science and engineering
  • Cultivate their curiosity about technology and discover it's interdisciplinary applications
  • Become confident in their abilities to learn independently
  • Gain interpersonal skills, teamwork experience, and confidence that will shape their future academic and professional endeavors
