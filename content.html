The Teknowledge Curriculum

A Computer Programming Curriculum desgined to engage students

Our executive board has designed a computer science and engineering curriculum to introduce students who have never written a line of code to Computer Science and Engineering and teach them the fundamentals through exciting labs. Each lab is designed to impart a specific set of skills and reinforce previously taught skills. Ultimately, the curriculum will equip students to fully build a technically challenging product of their own choice.
